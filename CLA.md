## How to sign

You sign this Agreement **once**, and it then covers all of your future
Contributions to the Project.

When you open your first pull request, the CLA Assistant bot will post a comment
asking you to sign. To sign, reply to that pull request with a comment
containing exactly:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

The bot records your GitHub username and the pull request in
[`signatures/version1/cla.json`](signatures/version1/cla.json). Once recorded,
you will not be asked again for future pull requests.
